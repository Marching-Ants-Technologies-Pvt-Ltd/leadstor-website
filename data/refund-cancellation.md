Thank you for choosing Leadstor for your payment processing needs. We understand that sometimes circumstances change, and you may need to request a refund or cancel a transaction. To make this process as smooth as possible, please review our Refund and Cancellation Policy outlined below.

# Refund Policy

### 1. Eligibility for Refunds

We strive to provide you with excellent service and support. Refunds may be considered under the following circumstances:

- **Duplicate Payments:** In the event of accidental duplicate payments, we will refund the duplicate amount promptly upon verification.
- **Service Disruptions:** If our payment processing service experiences a significant disruption or outage that results in transaction errors, we will work to refund any affected transactions or fees.

### 2. Refund Processs

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

- **Contact Customer Support:** Reach out to our Customer Support team via email or phone with your refund request. Be sure to provide all necessary transaction details for faster processing.
- **Verification: Our team will review your request and may request additional information to verify the transaction and the reason for the refund.
- **Approval:** Once your refund request is approved, we will initiate the refund process. Refunds will typically be issued to the original payment method used for the transaction.
- **Notification:** You will receive an email notification confirming the refund, including the refund amount and expected processing time.

### 3. Refund Timeframe

The time it takes for a refund to be processed may vary depending on your payment method and the specific circumstances of your request. Generally, refunds should be processed within [4 business weeks] after approval.

# Cancellation Policy

### 1. Transaction Cancellation

You have the right to cancel a transaction before it is processed. To cancel a transaction, follow these steps:

- **Contact Customer Support:** Reach out to our Customer Support team as soon as possible with your cancellation request and provide the necessary transaction details.
- **Verification:** Our team will review your request and verify if the transaction is eligible for cancellation.
- **Confirmation:** If your cancellation request is approved, you will receive an email confirmation of the cancellation, and no funds will be deducted or processed for the specified transaction.

### 2. Subscription Cancellation

If you have subscribed to any of our services, you may cancel your subscription at any time. Please keep the following in mind:

- **Billing Cycles:** Subscription cancellations will take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.
- **No Pro-Rated Refunds:** We do not provide pro-rated refunds for subscription cancellations.
